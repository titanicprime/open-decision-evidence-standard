# Code of Conduct

Contributors and maintainers are expected to participate in a professional, respectful, and constructive manner.

## Expected behavior

- Focus on technical issues, evidence, and clear reasoning.
- Treat participants with respect, even when disagreeing.
- Welcome good-faith contributions from different sectors, disciplines, and viewpoints.
- Avoid harassment, discrimination, intimidation, and personal attacks.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment or abusive language.
- Deliberate disruption of discussions.
- Misrepresentation of others' views or affiliations.
- Sharing confidential or private information without permission.

## Maintainer responsibility

Maintainers may moderate discussions, request changes in conduct, or remove contributions that do not meet these standards.
