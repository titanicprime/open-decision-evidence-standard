# Regulated Enterprise Profile

This profile sketches additional expectations that a regulated enterprise may impose on top of the base schema.

Expected additions or stricter practices include:

- explicit policy basis references suitable for internal and external review;
- authority validity windows rather than authority status alone;
- a populated human disposition reflecting actual review posture;
- an evidence commitment that supports later audit or controlled disclosure;
- an explicit expiration time;
- a verification method suitable for enterprise control environments; and
- purpose declarations that support audit and supervisory review.

Sector-specific regulatory obligations remain outside the scope of the base standard and should be layered through profile-specific requirements.
